Fine-tuning BERT for more complicated Tasks14
Bidirectional language models like BERT are very ﬂexible!Usually trained to perform text classiﬁcation§by ﬁne-tuning to replace “[CLS]” token by classBut can also be ﬁne-tuned to perform sequence labelling§by simply replacing the output text during ﬁne-tuning§e.g. with sequences of begin/inside/outside labelsMoreover, can be ﬁne-tuned to perform text pair classiﬁcation§by adding a special “[SEP]” token to separate 2 pieces of text§comparing texts is extremely useful for all sorts of applications §such as determining whether the statements agree with each otherBERT can be ﬁne-tuned for question-answering§but GPT is more appropriate model for that task
